### What Makes Sentinel Pay Different

| Feature | Sentinel Pay | Legacy Systems | Generic Gateways |
|---------|------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Fraud Detection** | 10-factor real-time scoring (<300ms) | Rules-based, slow | Basic velocity checks |

| **Transparency** | Detailed audit logs, clear APIs | Black box processing | Limited visibility |

### Unique Selling Points (USPs)

1. **Security-First Design** - Every feature designed with security as primary concern, not afterthought
2. **Real-Time Fraud Detection** - <300ms scoring with 10 intelligent factors (not just rules)
3. **Transparent Operations** - Complete audit trails, detailed dashboards, full API access
4. **Flexible Settlement** - Merchants control when/how they receive payouts
5. **Developer-Friendly** - Clear docs, SDKs in multiple languages, transparent pricing
6. **Enterprise-Grade** - 99.9%+ uptime, redundancy, compliance ready
